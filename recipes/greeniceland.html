Green Iceland


Description

A refreshing, dreamy blend that feels like sipping a breeze from a magical island. Green Iceland combines a crisp green apple base with the delicate sweetness of lychee syrup, all shaken with fragrant green tea for a balanced, icy-fresh flavor.
