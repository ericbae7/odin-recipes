McLoving


Description

A sweet, heartfelt blend made to share (or keep all to yourself). McLoving combines bright raspberry syrup, juicy mango syrup, and fragrant lychee syrup, all smoothed out with refreshing green tea. Lush, fruity, and perfectly balanced, this drink is bursting with love in every sip.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 raspberry syrup OZ
  • 1.5 OZ mango syrup
  • 1.2 OZ lychee syrup
  • Green tea

Steps

  1. Get a blender
  2. Get weighted scale
  3. Measure syrup base
  4. Measure side syrup
  5. Add tea that is needed
  6. Blend the tea
  7. Put in toppings first inside cup add ice then add tea mixture
  8. Seal drink
