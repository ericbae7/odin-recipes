Peach Party

Description

A sweet celebration in every sip. Peach Party blends rich peach syrup with smooth, aromatic oolong tea, creating a juicy, fragrant drink with a warm tea finish. Lightly floral, perfectly sweet, and irresistibly refreshing — it’s a little peachy party you won’t want to miss.

Ingredients

  • 4.5 peach syrup
  • Oolong tea

Steps

  1. Get a blender
  2. Get weighted scale
  3. Measure syrup base
  4. Measure side syrup
  5. Add tea that is needed
  6. Blend the tea
  7. Put in toppings first inside cup add ice then add tea mixture
  8. Seal drink
