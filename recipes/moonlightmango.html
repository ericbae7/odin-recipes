Moonlight Mango


Description

A serene blend of mango and peach, accented with green tea for a smooth, tropical tranquility.

Ingredients

  • 2.8 OZ mango syrup base
  • 1.4 OZ peach syrup
  • green tea

Steps

  1. Get a blender
  2. Get weighted scale
  3. Measure syrup base
  4. Measure side syrup
  5. Add tea that is needed
  6. Blend the tea
  7. Put in toppings first inside cup add ice then add tea mixture
  8. Seal the drink, all done~~
